As a member of a gym, you always want to get the most out of your visits to the gym. You don't want to spend ages waiting for the equipment you want to use to become free. You don't want go all the way to gym to attend a class that is actually full or cancelled. You want to know everything that is happening at your gym. This application is here to help you with all of this.

  • Book your class spot - Make sure you can attend the classes you want by booking your spot. You do this by going into the details of a class and pressing the "Book Class" button.
  • See how many people are at the gym - Make informed decisions about whether to go to gym by seeing how much people are currently there. You can see this number on your welcome screen.
  • Be informed - You will always know what's happening at your gym. The managers can send announcements informing you of what's happening. How are you informed? Either by email or by push notifications. You can also view all the announcements ever made by going to the announcements page. You are also informed by email if a class you have booked for has been cancelled.
